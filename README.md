## 📌 Overview
This project is a cybersecurity awareness training module focused on educating users about phishing attacks and how to stay protected from online threats.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Understand phishing attacks and social engineering tactics  
- Identify fake emails and malicious websites  
- Build awareness about cyber threats  
- Promote safe online behavior  

---

## 📚 Topics Covered
- Phishing email detection techniques  
- Fake website identification  
- Social engineering methods  
- Best security practices  

---

## 🎓 Learning Outcome
Users can recognize phishing attempts and avoid falling victim to cyberattacks by applying safe browsing habits.

---

## 🧪 Includes
- Real-world phishing examples  
- Safety tips and prevention methods  
- Interactive quiz for learning
